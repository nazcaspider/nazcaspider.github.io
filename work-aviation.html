Aviation

I grew up next to a small airfield (ICAO: SPTQ) high in the mountains of Toquepala-Peru and since then I've been obsessed with flying machines, which was the motivation for building drones, getting my pilot's license and flying a Cirrus SR22. Most recently I've been building a Velocity Experimental Aircraft and to help with that I attended the Composite Material and Mold Making courses at DarkAero.
